# Requirements Document

## Introduction

San Gabriel Solutions requires a comprehensive website repositioning to establish the company as a Vehicle Advertising & Mobile Visibility Solutions provider. The flagship product is Rear Window Vehicle Advertising (Perforated Vinyl). All messaging must support the core message: "Advertise While You Drive". The website must feature transparent pricing, clear conversion paths, and compliance with Ontario safety regulations. This document supersedes all previous website-related positioning.

## Glossary

- **San_Gabriel_Website**: The complete website for San Gabriel Solutions
- **Home_Page**: Landing page with vehicle advertising hero, pricing visibility, and primary CTAs
- **Services_Page**: Page presenting vehicle advertising services as dominant, with secondary services below
- **Pricing_Page**: Dedicated page displaying all pricing cards and bundles with compliance footnotes
- **Portfolio_Page**: Visual showcase of vehicle advertising work with mockups and before/after visuals
- **Contact_Page**: Quote-focused form with vehicle type, location, industry, and logo upload fields
- **Careers_Page**: Commission-only sales recruitment page
- **Rear_Window_Ad**: Flagship product using perforated vinyl for rear window advertising ($349)
- **Vehicle_Side_Ad**: Side or door advertising placement ($299 per side)
- **Hood_Branding**: Vehicle hood advertising placement ($449)
- **Real_Estate_Starter_Pack**: Bundle package for realtors ($699)
- **Professional_Vehicle_Package**: Comprehensive vehicle advertising bundle ($999)
- **Straight_Truck_Package**: Commercial truck visibility package ($3,299)
- **Perforated_Vinyl**: See-through vinyl material required for rear windows (safety compliant)
- **3M_IJ35_Blockout_Vinyl**: Required material for all non-rear window placements
- **CTA**: Call-to-action button driving user engagement

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Home Page Hero Repositioning

**User Story:** As a visitor landing on the homepage, I want to immediately understand that San Gabriel Solutions specializes in vehicle advertising, so that I can quickly determine if they offer what I need.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user lands on the Home_Page THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL display a hero headline: "Advertise While You Drive"
2. WHEN a user views the hero section THEN the Home_Page SHALL display a subheading: "Turn Your Vehicle Into a 24/7 Moving Billboard"
3. WHEN a user views the hero section THEN the Home_Page SHALL display two primary CTA buttons: "Get My Vehicle Wrapped" and "Get Realtor Package"
4. WHEN a user scrolls below the hero THEN the Home_Page SHALL feature the Rear_Window_Ad as the "Best Seller" product
5. WHEN a user views the homepage THEN the Home_Page SHALL remove or push down unrelated consulting services

### Requirement 2: Pricing Transparency

**User Story:** As a potential customer, I want to see clear pricing without excessive scrolling, so that I can quickly evaluate if the services fit my budget.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user views pricing information THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL display pricing cards with exact amounts
2. WHEN displaying the Rear_Window_Ad THEN the Pricing_Page SHALL show $349 with a "Best Seller" badge
3. WHEN displaying Vehicle_Side_Ad THEN the Pricing_Page SHALL show $299 per side
4. WHEN displaying Hood_Branding THEN the Pricing_Page SHALL show $449
5. WHEN displaying Real_Estate_Starter_Pack THEN the Pricing_Page SHALL show $699
6. WHEN displaying Professional_Vehicle_Package THEN the Pricing_Page SHALL show $999
7. WHEN displaying Straight_Truck_Package THEN the Pricing_Page SHALL show $3,299
8. WHEN pricing is displayed THEN the Pricing_Page SHALL explain what is included (installation, design)
9. WHEN pricing is displayed THEN the Pricing_Page SHALL include compliance footnotes about materials and safety

### Requirement 3: Services Page Vehicle Advertising Focus

**User Story:** As a visitor browsing services, I want to see vehicle advertising options prominently displayed first, so that I understand this is San Gabriel's primary offering.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user navigates to the Services_Page THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL display Vehicle Advertising as the first and dominant section
2. WHEN displaying vehicle advertising THEN the Services_Page SHALL clearly explain: Rear Window Ads, Side/Door Ads, Hood Branding, Truck & Trailer Advertising
3. WHEN displaying other services THEN the Services_Page SHALL move them to a secondary section below vehicle advertising
4. WHEN a user views vehicle advertising options THEN the Services_Page SHALL include pricing for each option
5. WHEN a user views the Services_Page THEN the Services_Page SHALL NOT display services with equal prominence

### Requirement 4: Material and Safety Compliance

**User Story:** As a customer concerned about safety and legality, I want to understand the materials used and compliance standards, so that I can trust the installation is safe and legal.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN displaying rear window advertising THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL specify that Perforated_Vinyl is the ONLY material used for rear windows
2. WHEN displaying non-rear window placements THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL specify that 3M_IJ35_Blockout_Vinyl is used
3. WHEN explaining materials THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL state that no solid vinyl is used on rear windows
4. WHEN displaying compliance information THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL reference Ontario visibility and safety regulations
5. WHEN displaying any advertising claims THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL NOT include guaranteed performance claims

### Requirement 5: Portfolio Page Visual Proof

**User Story:** As a potential customer evaluating quality, I want to see examples of vehicle advertising work, so that I can assess the quality and style of San Gabriel's work.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user navigates to the Portfolio_Page THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL display vehicle advertising visuals prominently
2. WHEN displaying portfolio items THEN the Portfolio_Page SHALL include before/after vehicle visuals where available
3. WHEN displaying mockups or concepts THEN the Portfolio_Page SHALL clearly label them as "Sample" or "Concept"
4. WHEN portfolio items are displayed THEN the Portfolio_Page SHALL show a variety of vehicle types (cars, trucks, trailers)
5. WHEN portfolio items are displayed THEN the Portfolio_Page SHALL be acceptable to launch with vehicle mockups

### Requirement 6: Quote-Focused Contact Form

**User Story:** As a visitor ready to get a quote, I want a contact form tailored to vehicle advertising, so that I can provide relevant information for an accurate quote.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user navigates to the Contact_Page THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL display a quote-focused form instead of a generic contact form
2. WHEN displaying the form THEN the Contact_Page SHALL include a field for Vehicle Type
3. WHEN displaying the form THEN the Contact_Page SHALL include a field for Location (GTA)
4. WHEN displaying the form THEN the Contact_Page SHALL include a field for Industry with "Real Estate" as the default option
5. WHEN displaying the form THEN the Contact_Page SHALL include a Logo Upload field
6. WHEN a user submits the form THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL capture all fields for quote generation

### Requirement 7: Careers Page Commission-Only Positioning

**User Story:** As a potential sales recruit, I want to understand the compensation structure clearly, so that I can determine if the opportunity fits my goals.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user navigates to the Careers_Page THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL display commission-only role information
2. WHEN displaying compensation THEN the Careers_Page SHALL NOT include any salary language
3. WHEN displaying roles THEN the Careers_Page SHALL focus on sales-focused recruitment messaging
4. WHEN displaying opportunities THEN the Careers_Page SHALL emphasize earning potential through commissions

### Requirement 8: Primary CTA Consistency

**User Story:** As a visitor on any page, I want consistent and clear calls-to-action, so that I always know how to take the next step.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN displaying primary CTAs THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL use: "Get My Vehicle Wrapped", "Advertise While You Drive", or "Get Realtor Package"
2. WHEN CTAs are displayed THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL NOT display duplicate CTAs on the same page section
3. WHEN a user clicks a CTA THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL navigate to the appropriate quote form or pricing page
4. WHEN displaying CTAs THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL include visual badges (e.g., "Best Seller") where appropriate

### Requirement 9: Mobile-First Responsive Design

**User Story:** As a mobile user, I want the website to be optimized for mobile viewing, so that I can easily browse and request quotes from my phone.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user accesses the San_Gabriel_Website on mobile THEN all pages SHALL display mobile-first responsive layouts
2. WHEN viewing pricing on mobile THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL display pricing visible without excessive scrolling
3. WHEN interacting on mobile THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL provide touch-friendly buttons and forms
4. WHEN viewing the hero section on mobile THEN the Home_Page SHALL maintain clear CTA visibility

### Requirement 10: Content Cleanup and De-prioritization

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want a focused website experience without clutter, so that I can quickly find vehicle advertising information.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN displaying the homepage THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL remove homepage clutter unrelated to vehicle advertising
2. WHEN displaying content THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL de-emphasize consulting language throughout
3. WHEN displaying content THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL remove vague buzzwords
4. WHEN displaying CTAs THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL eliminate duplicate CTAs within the same section

### Requirement 11: Bundle Highlighting

**User Story:** As a customer looking for value, I want to see bundle options clearly highlighted, so that I can choose a package that offers the best value for my needs.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN displaying pricing THEN the Pricing_Page SHALL highlight bundle packages (Real_Estate_Starter_Pack, Professional_Vehicle_Package, Straight_Truck_Package)
2. WHEN displaying bundles THEN the Pricing_Page SHALL explain what each bundle includes
3. WHEN displaying the Real_Estate_Starter_Pack THEN the Pricing_Page SHALL position it as ideal for realtors
4. WHEN displaying bundles THEN the Pricing_Page SHALL show savings compared to individual services where applicable

### Requirement 12: QA and Staging Approval Process

**User Story:** As a stakeholder, I want all changes verified against this document before going live, so that the website accurately reflects approved positioning and pricing.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN changes are made THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL require staging link submission for review
2. WHEN reviewing staging THEN all pricing and copy SHALL be checked against this requirements document
3. WHEN approving changes THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL require written approval before going live
4. WHEN displaying pricing THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL match the exact amounts specified in this document
