# Requirements Document

## Introduction

San Gabriel Solutions requires a comprehensive Services section that positions the company as a traffic monetization and advertising infrastructure provider. The Services section must explain all services offered, articulate the business case behind each service, demonstrate how services generate revenue, and position Traffic Monetization & Advertising Infrastructure as the core differentiator. The core business model anchors on: Traffic → Visibility → Monetization.

## Glossary

- **San_Gabriel_Services_Page**: The main Services page displaying all service categories in the required order
- **Traffic_Monetization**: The process of converting high-traffic physical and digital environments into revenue-generating assets
- **Service_Card**: A UI component displaying service information including title, description, business case, and CTA
- **Portfolio_Section**: Visual showcase of executed work including windows, trucks, banners, websites, and events
- **Lead_Form**: Contact form for requesting quotes, consultations, or partnerships
- **CTA**: Call-to-action button that drives user engagement

## Requirements

### Requirement 1

**User Story:** As a potential client visiting the Services page, I want to immediately understand San Gabriel Solutions' core value proposition around traffic monetization, so that I can see how they can help my business generate revenue.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user navigates to the Services page THEN the San_Gabriel_Services_Page SHALL display an introduction explaining that San Gabriel Solutions converts high-traffic environments into revenue-generating assets
2. WHEN a user views the Services page header THEN the San_Gabriel_Services_Page SHALL communicate the core model: Traffic → Visibility → Monetization
3. WHEN a user scrolls through services THEN the San_Gabriel_Services_Page SHALL display services in the exact required order: Revenue-Generating Advertising Solutions, Branding/Banners/Signs, Mobile Advertising, Monetize Your Location, Advertise With Us, Social Media & Digital, Website Design, Events & Community, Incorporation Services, Strategy/Technology/AI

### Requirement 2

**User Story:** As a business owner with high-traffic locations, I want to understand Revenue-Generating Advertising Solutions, so that I can turn my existing traffic into new income streams.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user views the Revenue-Generating Advertising Solutions section THEN the Service_Card SHALL explain that San Gabriel turns foot traffic, vehicle traffic, delivery routes, and digital attention into income streams
2. WHEN displaying the business case THEN the Service_Card SHALL state that digital advertising costs are rising, small businesses are priced out, physical traffic is under-monetized, and vulnerable businesses need additional revenue
3. WHEN showing service details THEN the Service_Card SHALL list: identify high-traffic locations, deploy physical and mobile advertising assets, match advertisers to locations, manage design/pricing/placement/monetization
4. WHEN a user views this section THEN the Service_Card SHALL include CTAs: "Turn My Traffic Into Revenue" and "Explore Advertising Options"

### Requirement 3

**User Story:** As a business needing physical advertising materials, I want to understand Branding, Banners & Signs services, so that I can get high-quality visual assets that drive revenue.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user views the Branding, Banners & Signs section THEN the Service_Card SHALL communicate that banners, signs, and visual branding are revenue-producing infrastructure
2. WHEN displaying services THEN the Service_Card SHALL list: roll-up banners, vinyl & mesh banners, step-and-repeat backdrops, storefront & window graphics, lawn signs & coroplast, posters & large-format signage, vehicle decals & wraps
3. WHEN showing design advantages THEN the Service_Card SHALL mention: designed for distance & motion, high-contrast layouts, QR codes & calls-to-action, print-ready accuracy
4. WHEN a user views this section THEN the Service_Card SHALL include CTA: "Request Banner & Sign Quote"

### Requirement 4

**User Story:** As a trucking company owner, I want to understand Mobile Advertising opportunities, so that I can earn additional revenue from my fleet.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user views the Mobile Advertising section THEN the Service_Card SHALL explain that short-haul and LTL trucks are moving billboards on high-traffic routes
2. WHEN displaying the business case THEN the Service_Card SHALL state that trucking margins are thin, visibility exists but is unused, and advertising offsets fuel/insurance/operating costs
3. WHEN showing advertising formats THEN the Service_Card SHALL list: vinyl wraps (full or partial), trailer-side panels, rear-door ads, magnetic signage, QR-enabled ads
4. WHEN displaying pricing THEN the Service_Card SHALL clearly state: $300–$500 per truck or trailer, depending on size, route, and duration
5. WHEN a user views this section THEN the Service_Card SHALL include CTA: "Earn With My Trucks"

### Requirement 5

**User Story:** As a visitor ready to engage with San Gabriel Solutions, I want clear final CTAs at the bottom of the Services page, so that I can easily choose how to participate.

2. WHEN displaying final CTAs THEN the San_Gabriel_Services_Page SHALL show three options: "Monetize My Location", "Advertise My Business", "Earn With My Trucks"
3. IF a user clicks any final CTA THEN the Lead_Form SHALL open with the appropriate service pre-selected

### Requirement 14

**User Story:** As a user browsing the Services page on any device, I want the page to be fully responsive and performant, so that I have a smooth experience regardless of how I access it.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user accesses the Services page on mobile devices THEN the San_Gabriel_Services_Page SHALL display a responsive layout with stacked service cards and touch-friendly buttons
2. WHEN the Services page loads THEN the San_Gabriel_Services_Page SHALL achieve fast load times through optimized assets and lazy loading
3. WHEN a user scrolls through service sections THEN the San_Gabriel_Services_Page SHALL display smooth animations and transitions
4. WHEN a user hovers over interactive elements THEN the San_Gabriel_Services_Page SHALL provide visual feedback through hover effects
