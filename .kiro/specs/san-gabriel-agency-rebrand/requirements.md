# Requirements Document

## Introduction

San Gabriel Solutions requires a comprehensive website rebrand to position the company as a professional marketing and advertising agency. The website must establish credibility and authority, clearly communicate services and value proposition, and generate consultation and discovery call inquiries. The brand should communicate strategic thinking, professionalism, trustworthiness, results-focus, and modern scalability. The primary conversion goal is scheduling consultation or discovery calls.

## Glossary

- **San_Gabriel_Website**: The complete website for San Gabriel Solutions
- **Home_Page**: Landing page with hero, services overview, differentiators, and proof of value
- **About_Page**: Page building trust through mission, philosophy, and approach
- **Services_Page**: Overview page presenting all five core service offerings
- **Service_Detail_Page**: Individual page for each service with detailed information
- **Industries_Section**: Section demonstrating relevance across business sectors
- **Case_Studies_Section**: Section showcasing client success stories with measurable outcomes
- **Insights_Page**: Thought leadership blog with marketing strategy content
- **Contact_Page**: Lead conversion page with inquiry form and discovery call CTA
- **Lead_Form**: Professional inquiry form for consultation requests
- **CTA**: Call-to-action button driving user engagement toward consultation

## Requirements

### Requirement 1

**User Story:** As a potential client visiting the homepage, I want to immediately understand San Gabriel Solutions' value proposition, so that I can quickly determine if they can help my business.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user lands on the Home_Page THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL display a hero section with a clear value proposition positioning San Gabriel as a strategic marketing and advertising partner
2. WHEN a user views the homepage hero THEN the Home_Page SHALL communicate authority, professionalism, and results-driven execution
3. WHEN a user scrolls the homepage THEN the Home_Page SHALL display sections in order: hero, services overview, differentiators, proof of value, primary CTA
4. WHEN a user views the homepage THEN the Home_Page SHALL maintain an executive-level, confident, and concise tone throughout
5. WHEN a user reaches any section THEN the Home_Page SHALL include clear CTAs directing users to schedule a consultation

### Requirement 2

**User Story:** As a business owner evaluating marketing agencies, I want to see an overview of services offered, so that I can understand the breadth of capabilities.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user views the services overview section THEN the Home_Page SHALL display high-level, strategic summaries of all five core services
2. WHEN displaying services THEN the Home_Page SHALL present: Marketing Strategy & Planning, Brand Development & Positioning, Advertising & Campaign Management, Digital Presence & Growth, Business & Marketing Consulting
3. WHEN a user views a service summary THEN the Home_Page SHALL include a link or button to view detailed service information
4. WHEN presenting services THEN the Home_Page SHALL emphasize integrated marketing and advertising aligned with business goals

### Requirement 3

**User Story:** As a potential client, I want to understand why I should choose San Gabriel Solutions over competitors, so that I can make an informed decision.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user views the differentiators section THEN the Home_Page SHALL communicate unique value propositions that distinguish San Gabriel Solutions
2. WHEN displaying differentiators THEN the Home_Page SHALL emphasize strategy-first approach, ethical data-driven results, and long-term partnerships
3. WHEN a user reads differentiators THEN the Home_Page SHALL avoid sales-heavy or promotional language while maintaining confidence

### Requirement 4

**User Story:** As a business decision-maker, I want to see proof of San Gabriel's capabilities, so that I can trust their expertise.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user views the proof section THEN the Home_Page SHALL display metrics, outcomes, or industry experience demonstrating credibility
2. WHEN showing proof THEN the Home_Page SHALL include trust signals such as industries served or measurable results
3. WHEN a user views proof elements THEN the Home_Page SHALL link to detailed case studies or insights for deeper validation

### Requirement 5

**User Story:** As a visitor wanting to learn about the company, I want to access an About page, so that I can understand San Gabriel's mission and approach.

### Requirement 6

**User Story:** As a potential client researching services, I want to view a comprehensive Services page, so that I can understand all offerings in detail.

3. WHEN displaying content THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL include trust signals such as case studies, experience, and insights
4. WHEN implementing pages THEN the San_Gabriel_Website SHALL follow SEO best practices for structure, metadata, and content
